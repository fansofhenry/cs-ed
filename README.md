# Henry Fan — CS Education Research Portfolio

Research portfolio site for Henry Fan, McNair Scholar at San José State University and Research Lead at the Foothill College Science Learning Institute.

**Live site:** [fansofhenry.github.io/cs-ed-research](https://fansofhenry.github.io/cs-ed-research/)

## Research Focus

Structural features of introductory CS courses that predict help-seeking, persistence, and belonging — with a focus on community college students.

## Site Structure

- `index.html` — Homepage and research identity
- `research.html` — Research agenda and four core questions
- `projects.html` — Five research projects (P1–P5) with full designs
- `writing.html` — Research statement, working papers, annotated reading list
- `tools.html` — Prototype tools and applied curriculum designs
- `background.html` — Origin story and contact
- `applied.html` — ProjectBridge and research-informed design work
- `style.css` — Shared stylesheet
